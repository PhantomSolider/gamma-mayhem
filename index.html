Mission Brief

Enter the Gamma Belt

Pilot the Core through warning lanes, falling shards, gravity wells, and black holes. Collect Phi Orbs to charge your pulse.

Controls

Move: WASD or Arrow Keys

Phi Pulse: Space

Pause: P

Systems

Gravity wells and black holes pull your route.

How it was made

A lightweight browser game built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Canvas.

Warning Lanes

Gamma rays mark their path before they fire. Move early and stay calm.

Gravity Pull

Wells and black holes tug at the Core. Correct your line before they drag you into danger.

Phi Pulse

Phi Orbs charge a burst that clears nearby hazards when you need space.

How to Play

Objective: Survive as long as possible and earn a high score.

Movement: Use WASD or the Arrow Keys.

Phi Orbs: Collect them to charge the Phi Pulse.

Phi Pulse: Press Space when charged to clear nearby danger or protect yourself.

Hazards: Dodge gamma rays, entropy shards, gravity wells, and black holes.

Pause: Press P.

Restart: Press Enter after game over.

Touch controls: Use the on-screen buttons on mobile.
